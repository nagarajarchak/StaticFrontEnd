7 BILLION & YOU

Every second, millions of bytes of information are shared across more sophisticated
networks than have ever existed. Yet, the mismatch of a pixel has the
ability to bring down colossal systems: all it takes is a spark.

There are billions of us, but there's only one of you.
What are you doing today?
